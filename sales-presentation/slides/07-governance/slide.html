Akka Governance Platform
Observation is not control.
Moving Target
174 regulatory frameworks — all of them changing. Which ones apply to your agents? How do you re-posture every time they shift? EU AI Act penalties alone reach €35M.
Unenforceable
Governance tools observe AI from the outside. They can't enforce at agent boundaries, can't gate a deployment, can't link a policy decision to the human who approved it. They produce reports — not proof.
PB+
Petabyte-scale storage for regulatory holds — 10-year retention for high-risk AI under EU AI Act Art. 72. Copied logs aren't tamper-proof. When auditors come, you need hash-chains — not hope. Governance isn't cheap; it has to be built in.
One platform governs what you build and what you run — and stays in sync as regulations change.
An AI platform with a living regulatory corpus — enforced at runtime.
Akka-Native
Full interior enforcement — event journal, tool metadata, session memory, delegation chains. Every decision logged.
Hybrid
Akka proxy in the call path for external agents — OpenAI, Anthropic, LangChain, CrewAI. Wire-level signals: prompts, responses, tokens, timing.
Proxy-Only
No instrumentation required. Governance via signed attestation documents — Model Cards, DPIAs, Red-Team Reports, SOC 2.

Living Regulatory Corpus

03 / 08
Governance Posture Dashboard
Article-by-article regulatory coverage mapped to every deployed system in real time. 174 frameworks. 521 controls. Violations, halted executions, rubber-stamped HITL, and scope drift — surfaced the moment they happen, not reconstructed from logs after the fact.
04 / 08
Immutable Coordination Log
Every agent coordination event — delegation, tool call, HITL gate, LLM invocation — captured automatically by the runtime. Zero developer instrumentation. Hash-chained for tamper-proof evidence. EU AI Act Art. 12 retention tiers built in. This is the source of truth.
05 / 08
Policy Authoring & Sign-off
Governance is a deployment pre-condition, not an afterthought. Each control maps to exact regulatory articles, specifies runtime enforcement requirements (guardrails, observability), and requires sign-off from every relevant persona — Legal, Ethics, Model Risk, Security. No signed bundle, no deployment.
06 / 08
Bring Your Monitors
34 types of outcome metrics — PSI, AUC degradation, Gini, bias ratios, and more — evaluated against live rolling-window feeds. Akka provides baseline probes, but more importantly integrates with your existing monitoring stack and the best-in-class third-party monitors your organization has already chosen. 135 tunable parameters. No lock-in.
07 / 08
Policy Sandbox
Test policy changes against every historical scenario before they go live. See exactly which agents break, which pass, and what regresses — against your real corpus of controls. Catch the blast radius of a regulatory update before it hits production.
08 / 08
Governance Posture Package
A signed, portable compliance artifact that travels with every deployment. Cryptographically links every human approval to the deployed system. Classification, coverage dossier, attestation documents, and version history — in one artifact your auditors, regulators, and board can verify.
